# Amplif.ai

> **Your block. Your people. Connected by AI.**

Amplif.ai is a hyperlocal neighbor-connection platform built for New York City. It uses AI matching to connect people on the same block by skills, hobbies, and music taste — turning strangers into neighbors and neighbors into a community.

🏆 **Won 2 consecutive hackathons**  
🌐 **[Live Demo →](https://home-block-amplified.lovable.app)**

---

## What It Does

- **Skill Swaps** — Find neighbors who have what you need and offer what you know
- **Direct Messaging** — Connect 1-on-1 with people on your block
- **Block Party Coordination** — Organize local events and meetups
- **AI Matching** — Intelligently surfaces the most relevant neighbors based on shared interests and complementary skills
- **Hyperlocal by Design** — Every connection is grounded in your physical community

The Story
Amplif.ai was born out of a simple observation: NYC is the most densely populated city in the US, yet most people don't know their neighbors. We built this at a hackathon in a single weekend — and won. Then we built it again, better, for a brooklyn Powerup competetion.

The idea is simple: your block is already full of talented, interesting people. Amplif.ai just makes the introduction.
